Character Entity Reference Examples

Special Characters:

Ampersand: &

Less-than sign: <

Greater-than sign: >

Double quotation mark: "

Single quotation mark: '

Symbols:

Copyright symbol: ©

Registered trademark: ®

Trademark symbol: ™

Euro symbol: €

Pound symbol: £

Yen symbol: ¥

Cent symbol: ¢

Mathematical Symbols:

Degree symbol: °

Plus-minus symbol: ±

Multiplication sign: ×

Division sign: ÷

Infinity symbol: ∞

Not equal to: ≠

Less than or equal to: ≤

Greater than or equal to: ≥

Greek Letters:

Alpha: α

Beta: β

Pi: π

Summation symbol: ∑
